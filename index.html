Our Loveline

This is a story of boy meets girl, and you should know upfront, this is a love story.

The first day we met

21 APR, 2011

This is up for debate, but it is simplest to say that our romance began in the most classy of scenarios - Mug Night at Whitlows. It was Renae's birthday and Valerie was out with the girls to celebrate. Nick started out at another bar that night, but fortunately ended up right where he needed to be. Nick and Valerie quickly hit it off, even coming up with a little inside joke (kick high five!) before the night was over.

Our first date

25 APR, 2011

We did the typical first date thing - dinner in Shirlington with some extra time spent walking around the area talking. But there was nothing typical about that night. After a whirlwind weekend of facebook messaging, texting, and meeting at a bar with friends, we had already fallen for each other. And the first date was just the icing on the cake - especially the part when we sang Katy Perry's "ET" in perfect unison.

The Proposal

2 Oct, 2014

After 4 years of dating and 2 years of sharing a roof, Nick finally made an honest girl out of Valerie one night in Virginia Beach. While Nick would like to think it was a big surprise, Valerie had a sneaking suspicion the "moment" was coming when Nick planned a wine picnic on the beach. After he popped the question, they got to spend the night celebrating with Valerie's family - exactly the way she always wanted.

The Wedding

4 JUN, 2016

After a few months of researching (nearly) every wedding venue in the DC metro area, we found the perfect spot to exchange "I Do's" at Sunset Crest Manor in Chantilly, VA. It will be just over 5 years since "Nickerie" was first established. We can't wait to share the most important moment in our love story with all the people we love most.
